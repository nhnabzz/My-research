Who am I?

I am a Senior Research Fellow at the Structural Biology Lab, Department of Biophysics, AIIMS, New Delhi. My research focuses on structural and functional studies of bacterial ATP phosphoribosyltransferase (ATPPRT) using X-ray crystallography.

Expertise

  • Protein crystallography
  • Structural virology
  • Cryo-EM (learning phase)
  • SAXS (learning phase)

Projects Undertaken

My research includes elucidating the 3D structures of key drug targets such as:

  • Phosphopantetheine adenylyltransferase (PPAT)
  • Adenosine triphosphate phosphoribosyltransferase (ATPPRT)

Summary of Results

I have successfully determined the crystal structures of HisG, PPAT, and ATPPRT. I am currently working on obtaining the 3D structure of the HisZ protein.
